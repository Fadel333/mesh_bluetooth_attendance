Core Technology
How ProxiMesh Works
📡
BLE Mesh Scanning
Student devices broadcast and scan Bluetooth Low Energy signals continuously, detecting nearby registered devices without internet.
🧠
Cluster Verification
The algorithm checks if the majority of detected nearby devices belong to the student's assigned group before approving attendance.
🔒
Anti-Spoofing Guards
Device registration, signal-strength filtering, group anchor beacons, and shake gestures prevent manipulation and spoofing.
📶
Offline Capable
Core proximity verification runs entirely over Bluetooth — no WiFi or mobile data required during the scanning phase.
📊
Real-time Dashboard
Lecturers see live group clusters, attendance counts, and flagged anomalies as they happen across every registered group.
⚓
Group Anchor Device
Each group has a designated anchor device broadcasting a signed beacon, forming a trusted center for the cluster mesh.
